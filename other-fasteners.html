Other Fasteners

Maharashtra Fastners is a leading supplier of a diverse range of fasteners, including specialized components beyond traditional nuts, bolts, screws, and washers. Located in Maharashtra, India, we specialize in providing innovative and high-quality fastening solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our customers across various industries. From rivets and clips to pins and anchors, we offer a comprehensive selection of fasteners designed to address a wide range of applications and requirements.

  • Benefits

  • Materials: We utilize a range of materials, including steel, stainless steel, aluminum, brass, and plastics, to manufacture fasteners that offer durability, corrosion resistance, and strength.
  • Specialized Designs: Our fasteners feature specialized designs and configurations tailored to meet specific application requirements, including unique shapes, sizes, and functionalities.
  • Customization Options: Maharashtra Fastners provides customization services for fasteners, allowing for tailored solutions to address unique project needs, including material selection, size specifications, surface finishes, and special features.

  • Standards Compliance: We adhere to international standards and specifications governing fasteners, ensuring that our products meet the highest quality and compatibility standards.
  • Quality Assurance: Maharashtra Fastners implements stringent quality control measures throughout the manufacturing process to ensure that our fasteners meet or exceed industry standards for performance, reliability, and durability.

  • Optimized Performance: By providing specialized designs and configurations, our fasteners enhance performance, reliability, and efficiency in fastening systems.
