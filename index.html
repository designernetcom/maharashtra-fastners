About Company

Your Trusted Partner for Quality Industrial Fasteners

Established in 1996, Maharashtra Fasteners Pvt. Ltd. is a renowned name in the manufacturing and supply of industrial fasteners. With a strong foundation built on a commitment to quality and innovation, we have grown to become a trusted partner to leading automobile and accessory manufacturers. Our comprehensive range of fasteners, including nuts, screws, bolts, and special parts, is backed by in-house technical expertise and a fully equipped manufacturing facility.

Certified with IATF16949, we ensure the highest standards of quality and reliability in every fastener we produce. At Maharashtra Fasteners Pvt. Ltd., we are dedicated to providing superior fastening solutions that meet the diverse needs of our customers.
